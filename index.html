ABOUT US

Fionn Medical Centre is dedicated to providing top-quality healthcare services with a patient-centered approach. Our commitment to continuous improvement ensures that we meet the evolving needs of our community with care, compassion, and excellence.

  • Experienced and compassionate medical professionals
  • Modern facilities with advanced medical equipment
  • Wide range of services including general care and specialist consultations
  • Accessible, welcoming, and inclusive environment
